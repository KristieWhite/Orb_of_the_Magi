ORB OF THE MAGI SERIES

by Dennis Durfey

DARK STAR

After completing the mysterious test to become a Grand Magi with her shandar, Jerdone, Zandreena was looking forward to peaceful years of studying to perfect her magic. But fate and a prophecy of old made her leave her home and led her to an adventure and eventful encounter with the fearless Ash'len, the spiritual Danica, and the proud thief Ren, all of whom would play a role in her journey to finding the Orb of the Magi. This immense duty placed in the hands of this young Grand Magi was not welcomed by everyone, and she had to protect not only her life and her friends' but also her very mission to locate the orb. With the land stripped of its grandeur and serenity due to wars and dark magic, these young men and women must stay together, fight for their lives, and believe that fate would direct them to saving mankind.

"a gripping and potent work of modern fiction with twists and turns that make this enthralling page turner an engrossing obsession from start to finish."

Fading Moons

Trekking their way across war-torn lands, Zandreena, the Dark Star, the Chosen One, and her brave prince, Ash'len, continue upon their quest to find the legendary Orb of the Magi, the Creation Stone. With the help of the young cleric, Danica, and the mysterious thief, Ren, they travel from the frozen majestic mountains of the north to the southern desert wastelands in search of clues needed to accomplish their mission. Meanwhile, as the war continues to rage across the kingdom of Sendrison, an internal power struggle for the right to be king threatens to tear the mighty kingdom apart from the inside. With the help of new friends and unlikely allies, Zandreena and her companions battle their way through humanoids and demons alike, all the while drawing ever closer to the fabled stone. As they continue on their quest, they begin to discover the truth about the mysterious orb and meaning of the ancient prophecies while learning about themselves and the role they must play in diverting the coming apocalypse to save all of mankind.
